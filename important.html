Important safety information

· Read all the safety information below before using your device.

· Using unauthorised cables, power adapters, or batteries can cause fire or explosion, or pose other risks.

· Use only authorised accessories which are compatible with your device.

· Use this device within the temperature range of 0°C to 40°C, and store this device and its accessories within the temperature range of -20°C to 45°C. Using this device in environments outside of this temperature range may damage the device.

· If your device is provided with a built-in battery, don't attempt to replace the battery yourself. This may damage the battery or the device. The battery may only be replaced at an authorised service center.

· Charge this device only with the included or authorised cable and power adapter. Using other adapters may cause fire, electric shock, and damage the device and the adapter.

· After charging is complete, disconnect the adapter from both the device and the power outlet. Do not charge the device for longer than 12 hours.

· Do not attempt to modify the plug or power cord on your own, and disconnect the power supply before cleaning the charger.

· Do not disassemble, hit, crush, or burn the battery. In case of swelling or other deformation, stop using the battery immediately.

· Do not short circuit the battery. This may result in overheating, burns, or other personal injuries.

· Do not place the battery u a high-temperature environment. Overheating may cause explosion.

· Do not disassemble, hit, or crush the battery to avoid battery leakage, overheating, or explosion.

· Do not burn the battery. This may result in fire or explosion

· In case of change in color or shape, swelling, unusually high temperature, or other deformation, stop using the battery immediately.

· Keep your device dry. Do not store or use the product and it accessories in a hot and humid environment or near an open fire.

· To prevent voiding the warranty, do not disassemble or modify the device and its accessories. If any part of the device does not work preperly, contact Xiaomi customer support or bring your device to an authorised repair center.

· To prevent possible hearing damage, do not listen at high volume levels for long periods.

· Before cleaning or conducting maintenance on this device, close all applications, stop using the device, and disconnect all other devices and cables.

· Use a soft, clean, dry cloth to wipe the device and its accessories. Do not use harse chemicals or detergents to clean the device ot its accessories.

· Do not use external heating equipment such as a microwave or hair dryer to dry the device and its accessories.

Child safety

· Keep the device and all accessories away from children. Do not allow children to play with, chew, or swallow the device or any of its accessories to avoid the risk of choking or suffocation.
